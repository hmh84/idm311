The Scene

Interaction Proposal

Menu system controllers

1. Checkbox toggle on click toggles background music.

2. A slider on drag changes the angle of the sun. This would allow the user to change the time of day and get different lighting effects (e.g. day, night, etc.)

3. Checkbox toggle on click toggles the visibility a moving stars skybox. This can only be seen at night.

Interactive 3D objects

1. Stone with the curled blue line next to the pond on click will drain or fill the pond.

2. The mountains will surround the floating islands. Clicking on any of them will raise them all above and below the Y level of the islands.

3. Lighting objects on click will toggle illumination on/off (this will mostly affect ambience at night).

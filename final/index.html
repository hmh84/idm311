IDM 331 Final: Secluded Sky Islands

Hunter Hampton

Interaction Guide

Interactive 3D Objects

1. Clicking on the stone with the curled blue line next to the pond will toggle if the pond is filled.

2. Clicking any of the mountains will raise them all above and below roughly the Y level (height) of the islands you're standing on.

3. Clicking on the torches will toggle their illumination on/off.

Menu System

1. "Auto Rotate" checkbox that toggles if the sun automatically rotates around the game area.

2. While the "Auto Rotate" checkbox is un-checked (off), a slider will appear allowing you to change the angle of the sun in the sky manually.

3. "Mute" checkbox toggles the background music.
